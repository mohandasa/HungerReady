Features

New Order:

This tab hosts functionalities that assist you with searching or adding new customers by tracking their phone numbers as IDs while filtering them by the order type i.e. Dine-In, Pick-Up or Delivery. It also lets you manage your restaurant's menu, process new orders and hosts functionalities that assist you with delivery management through integration with the HungeReadyToDrive? mobile application.

Monitor:

This tab hosts functionalities that assist you with monitoring your restaurant's orders from its preparation stage in the kitchen to its completion as Dine-In, Pick-Up or Delivery.

Settings:

This tab hosts functionalities that assist you with Account Overview and Logging Out of the system.
